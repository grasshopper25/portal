About Us

Welcome to Grasshopper — your trusted partner in guiding students toward their brightest futures. Based in Kolkata, we are a dedicated and passionate student counseling organization committed to helping aspiring learners navigate the world of higher education.

At Grasshopper, our mission is rooted in the noble goal of empowering students with the right information and personalized guidance to pursue professional courses across diverse universities and colleges, both within India and abroad. We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to explore their potential and make informed decisions about their academic and professional journeys.

Our team of experienced counselors works tirelessly to provide free, unbiased advice tailored to each student’s aspirations, strengths, and interests. Whether you're looking to study in a prestigious local college or dreaming of international exposure, Grasshopper is here to assist you every step of the way.

Join us in transforming dreams into reality. Together, we can build a brighter future for the leaders of tomorrow.

How it works

At Grasshopper, our process is simple, transparent, and designed to ensure you receive the best guidance tailored to your aspirations.
Here’s how we help students find their perfect educational path:
1
Reach Out to Us

Start by contacting us through our website or via phone. You can also fill out a simple form with your basic details and areas of interest

2
Schedule a Counseling Session

Our experienced counselors will get in touch to understand your academic background, career goals, interests, and preferences. We offer personalized, one-on-one guidance tailored to your needs.

3
Explore Your Options

Based on your profile, we present a range of suitable courses, universities, and colleges — both in India and abroad. We provide comprehensive information about admission requirements, course details, and future career prospects.

4
Make Informed Decisions

With our guidance, you can compare options, clarify doubts, and choose the best-fit course and institution that align with your ambitions and circumstances.

5
Support Throughout the Process

From application assistance to documentation and visa guidance (if applicable), we remain by your side to make the admission process smooth and stress-free.

6
Achieve Your Dream

Once admitted, we continue to support you with any follow-up guidance needed to ensure your educational journey begins successfully.

Remember: Our services are completely free, and our goal is to help you make the most informed decision for a bright future.
